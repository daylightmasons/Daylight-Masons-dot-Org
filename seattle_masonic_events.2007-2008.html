May 3 -              Special Meeting to confer Entered Apprentice Degree

May 17 -            Stated Meeting – last visit of Daylight’s own V\W\ James R. Maher and a special salute to be held

June 12-14       Grand Lodge meeting in Wenatchee

June 21-22       Fremont Solstice Festive and Street Fair with Daylight Bandstand and Seattle Masonic Information Booth 10:00 a. m. to about 8:00 p.m. both days

June 28 -          Stated Meeting - Election of 2008-2009 officers and to greet any new Grand Lodge officers

July 12 -           Special Meeting for the Public Installation of Officers by Deputy Grand Master R\W\ Gale H. Kenney

July 19 -           First Stated meeting of the 2008-2009 Masonic Year.

 

Stated Meetings are usually worked on the Entered Apprentice Degree.  Unless otherwise noted meetings start at 10:00 a.m.  There is a lunch following all meetings; stated meetings lunch is at the Masonic Hall, degree meetings lunch is at a local restaurant.
